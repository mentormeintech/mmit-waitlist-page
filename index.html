About Us

MentorMeInTech (MMIT) is a community driven platform that provides free mentorship and guidance to Africans transitioning into Tech. To bridge the gap between Africans interested in building a career in tech and willing professionals with expertise to guide them on their journey. We can accomplish these with the 3 keys: Mentoring sessions, Partnerships with Edutech platforms and an inclusive community.
